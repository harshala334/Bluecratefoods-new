## 💡 **Why React Native (Not Swift/Kotlin)?**

You asked about native development. Here's why React Native is better for BlueCrateFoods:

### React Native Wins:
- ✅ **Single codebase** → iOS + Android together
- ✅ **60% faster** development
- ✅ **Same patterns** as your Next.js web app
- ✅ **Your team already knows React**
- ✅ **Near-native performance** (good enough for food delivery)
- ✅ **Huge ecosystem** (maps, payments, notifications all supported)
- ✅ **Hot reload** (instant preview during development)

### When You'd Need Native:
- ❌ Heavy 3D graphics
- ❌ Complex AR/VR
- ❌ Apps like Instagram/TikTok (but even they use RN!)

**For BlueCrateFoods (e-commerce/delivery), React Native is PERFECT!** ✅
